Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence and Automation, Huazhong University of Science and Technology(HUST). Before that, I got the B.Eng. degree in Department of Physics and Electronics, Central South University(CSU). My research interests include computer vision, deep learning and machine learning.

My research field is motion estimation, domain adaptation and multi-modal fusion.
